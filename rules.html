The rules of street dice

  • PLAYERS
  • This game is normally played between two people , Player 1 rolls then Player 2 then the rolls are compared accordingly
  • 4–5–6
  • The highest possible roll. If you roll 4–5–6, you automatically win.
  • Tripples
  • Rolling three same numbers is known as rolling a triple. Higher triples beat lower triples, so 4–4–4 is better than 3–3–3. Any triple beats any established point.
  • Point
  • Rolling a pair, and another number, establishes the singleton as a "point". A higher point beats a lower point, so 2–2–6 is better than 5–5–2.
  • 1–2–3
  • The lowest possible roll. If you roll 1–2–3, you automatically lose.
  • Any other roll is a meaningless combination and must be rerolled until one of the above combinations occurs.
  • The game is played in rounds , so each player rolls, compares values then a winner is decided and a new round is begun.
